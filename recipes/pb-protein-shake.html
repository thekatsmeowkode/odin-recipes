Peanut Butter Protein Shake

This combination is so simple, I hardly consider it a recipe

    Ingredients
  • 1 frozen banana*
  • 3 tablespoons hemp hearts
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons raw cacao powder (or cocoa powder)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (alcohol-free, if desired)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    Instructions
  1. Combine all of the ingredients in a high-speed blender, and blend until thick and smooth.
  2. Adjust any flavors to taste, as desired, and serve immediately!
